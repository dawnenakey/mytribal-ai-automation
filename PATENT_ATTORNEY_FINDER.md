## üìã Questions to Ask Patent Attorney

### During Consultation:

1. **Experience:**
   - "How many software patents have you filed?"
   - "Do you have experience with AI/ML patents?"
   - "What's your approval rate?"

2. **Your Invention:**
   - "Do you think my invention is patentable?"
   - "What are the chances of approval?"
   - "What makes it novel?"

3. **Process:**
   - "What's the timeline?"
   - "What do you need from me?"
   - "How do we work together?"

4. **Costs:**
   - "What are your fees?"
   - "Is it hourly or fixed fee?"
   - "What are potential additional costs?"
   - "Can you provide cost estimate?"

5. **Strategy:**
   - "Should I file provisional first?"
   - "What's the best approach for my situation?"

## üéØ Recommended Approach

### Step 1: Research (Free)
- Search Google Patents yourself
- Understand what exists
- Prepare questions

### Step 2: Get 2-3 Consultations ($200-$500 each)
- Talk to multiple attorneys
- Compare advice
- Compare costs
- Find best fit

### Step 3: Choose Attorney
- Based on experience, cost, fit
- Start with provisional patent (lower risk)

### Step 4: File Provisional ($2K-$5K)
- Establishes priority
- Buy yourself time
- Lower cost entry

### Step 5: Decide on Full Patent Later
- After testing market
- After raising funds
- After refining invention
